What's all the fuss about?

As a group of college students, we are well acquainted with the task of creating meals on our own. When given the task of constructing something using API's from Target and Pearson, we noticed that Pearson had one containing scores of recipes. This gave us the idea that we might be able to turn this struggle into something great.


'insert name here' gives you access to countless recipes spanning a number of categories. Not only do you have access to these recipes, but through the use of an API provided by Target, you can see which ingredients are available to you at the nearest Target. This gives you the ability to check prices as well, allowing you to base your meals on the budget you are working with.


To provide further incentive to use our product, you will earn badges as you learn new skills and recipes via the Acclaim API provided by Pearson. This provides a sense of achievement to give users something to strive for. The more meals you learn to make, the more badges you get.
